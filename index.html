Hello! I'm Nuho. I earned my undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Engineering at the International Islamic University Chittagong in December 2023. During this time, I co-developed DiabSense, a novel smartphone-based system for early diagnosis of type 2 diabetes using Graph Neural Networks. This research led to my first peer-reviewed publication — Journal of Big Data (Q1, Impact Factor: 6.4).

I further explored the intersection of Machine Learning and real-world applications by working on contrastive clustering-based diabetic retinopathy grading to introduce new perspectives with reducing ophthalmologists' bias, and high-fidelity reconstruction of 3D temperature fields by proposing attention-augmented CNN autoencoder. My goal is to apply and advance machine learning/deep learning algorithms to address real-world challenges.
